	\textbf{Normative Analysis}
		\begin{itemize}
			\item Type I Transaction costs
      \begin{itemize}
        \item Buyers did not have multiple systems to pick from which reduced the costs dramatically
        \item On the verge to 2G the increased flexibility for the costumers encouraged the usage of 2G
      \end{itemize}
			\item Type II Transaction costs:
      \begin{itemize}
        % Costs of attracting complementary products and services
        \item Step by step a universal standard arose
        \item It is open to many manufacturers, but it was costly to get in later
      \end{itemize}
		\end{itemize}

	\textbf{Positive Analysis}
		\begin{itemize}
			\item The goals were reached
      \item Success Factors of 2g: Timing, size of consortium, mass production, lock in and international roaming
		\end{itemize}
